Aircon Servicing Contracts are Ideal for commercial Clients

Regular air conditioning maintenance will ensure that your equipment is not affected by a 5% reduction in the efficiency associated with the accumulation of dust and hot particles in your aircon.

Annual maintenance also helps to optimize performance and reduce the chance of damage which is caused by continuous heating inside the air conditioning unit.
