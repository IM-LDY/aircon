Service Pricing

Normal Servicing

Job :

  • Checking and cleaning the front panel & cover , air filter
  • Checking , pulify and deodorising filter
  • Checking and cleaning evaporator coil
  • Checking and cleaning drainage tray
  • Discharge pressure and checking compressor function

 

Chemical Wash

Job:
  • Checking and cleaning front panel/cover , air filter
  • Checking, purifying and deodorising air con filter
  • Checking and cleaning evaporator coil
  • Checking and cleaning drainage tray
  • Vacuum drainage system
  • Checking lubrication and fan bearing
  • Checking compressor suction and
  • Top up aircon gas (chargeable)
  • Electrical contacts tightening

 

Chemical Overhaul

Job:

  • Lubricates fan bearings to make sure the air con unit without producing any noise
  • Checks controls of the unit and thermostats
  • Cleans pipes, drain pans and blower wheel with chemicals
  • Cleans fan evaporator coil to facilitate smooth transfer of heat without the build up of dust and grime
  • Eliminates operational noise and replaces faulty bearings

Contract – Yearly Maintenance
